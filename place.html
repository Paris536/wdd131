Uganda — The Pearl of Africa

Explore vibrant culture, diverse wildlife, and breathtaking landscapes.

About

Uganda is landlocked in East Africa, famous for its mountains, lakes, and wildlife. Visitors enjoy safaris, cultural experiences, and mountain trekking.

Top places to visit

  • Murchison Falls — dramatic waterfalls and wildlife.
  • Kampala — the lively capital with markets and culture.
  • Bwindi — gorilla trekking in ancient forests.
  • Sipi Falls-for beautiful sceneries and mountain hiking
